Morya 2K18

        
            Morya 2K18 is the annual Intra-College event from the department of Computer Science.
            It is the Inter-College events in which students from different college can also participate along with the native college students.
            In Morya 2K18 there are many events as following :
                                                               1.Paper Presentation
                                                               2.Coding Master
                                                               3.Poster Presentation.

                    Winners for the Morya 2K18 for the year 2018 are as follows :

                    2.Coding Master : Trupti Kamble from VYMP.

                    3.Poster Presentation : Shubham Patil from AMGOI.
